Toad in the Hole

Toad in the Hole is a traditional British dish made with sausages baked in a Yorkshire pudding batter. It's a hearty and satisfying meal that's perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients:

  • flour
  • eggs
  • milk
  • salt
  • pepper
  • butter
  • red or white onions
  • beef stock
  • olive oil
  • sausages
